Kazo Child Development Centre creates a productive, health environment for young generation to thrive in the community to reach their full potential and to take control of their lives and be economically self-supporting through.

  • Support families to be economically self-supporting.
  • Empowering the children and youth
  • Providing access to education and functional skills.
  • Providing psychosocial support to families affected by HIV/AIDS.
  • Providing quality health services to the beneficiaries.
